About Us

Hallo! I’m Jonell Ebreo, creator of Fandom Fiction. Throughout our Digital Authoring course, I’ve been looking into an analyzing different ways to connect with digital audiences. Through my experiences this semester, I’ve refined my analysis of audience to focus on digital fandoms and the types of media they consume. By understanding and dissecting their preferred content, I’ve learned how to curate the kind of content I produce to be in-line with their preferences.

This isn’t to say that I’ve only created content based on what other people like. I was drawn towards fandom and fanfiction in the first place because that is the type of content I myself enjoy and have experience with. I’ve been writing and reading fanfiction for about 7 years now, so I’ve seen the type of content that is popular amongst readers.

This course has been a great way for me to develop my skills as a Digital Author while expressing my own interests about the world of digital fandoms.
